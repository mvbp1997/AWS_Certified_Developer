## Exam Outline
- 5 Domains:
  - Deployment (22%)
  - Security (26%)
  - Development with AWS Services (30%)
  - Refactoring (10%)
  - Monitoring and Troubleshooting (12%)
- Two types of questions: **multiple choice**, **multiple response**
